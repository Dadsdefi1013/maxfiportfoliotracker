DadsDeFiSpace — MaxFi Portfolio Dashboard
Paste your MaxFi position data to generate your personal dashboard. Works for any Base chain wallet. Nothing is stored or sent anywhere.
1
Open MaxFi at maxfi.tech and go to your positions page.
2
Use the DadsDeFiSpace Claude prompt — Get the community prompt ↗ · Screenshot or copy your MaxFi position cards, open Claude.ai, paste the prompt + your data, and Claude returns clean JSON automatically. Works with screenshots or copied text.
3
Paste JSON below and hit Generate. Your IL, APR, cashflow, risk, scenarios, and allocation render instantly.
4
Add compounding info — set autoCompound: true on positions that compound automatically. MaxFi compounds 50% to your wallet, 50% back into the position.
Field reference: pair, positionId, dex, daysOld, value, earned, uncollected, collected, apr, dpd, rebalances, inRange, autoCompound, tokenA, qtyA, entryPriceA, currentPriceA, tokenB, qtyB, entryPriceB, currentPriceB, rangeLower, rangeUpper, rangeCurrentPrice. DeFi Is High Risk
Decentralized finance (DeFi) is experimental technology. Providing liquidity in concentrated liquidity pools carries significant risks including but not limited to: impermanent loss, smart contract exploits, oracle manipulation, protocol insolvency, regulatory action, and total loss of deposited funds. Never deposit more than you can afford to lose completely.
All Metrics Are Projections
APR, $/day, annual yield, monthly yield, break-even days, and all scenario projections displayed on this dashboard are estimates based on current snapshot data. Past performance does not guarantee future results. Fee rates, pool volume, token prices, and rebalance frequency can change dramatically at any time.
Prices Are Estimates
Token prices shown are derived from on-chain ratios at the time of your snapshot. They are not live prices and may not reflect current market conditions. IL calculations, hold value, and net vs hold figures depend on these estimated prices and will change as markets move. Always verify current prices on CoinGecko or a live exchange.

Smart Contract Risk
Funds deposited into MaxFi and underlying DEX pools are controlled by smart contracts. Even audited smart contracts can contain bugs or be exploited. MaxFi's auto-rebalancing mechanism introduces additional smart contract interactions that increase surface area for potential failures. Understand the contracts before depositing.
