DadsDeFiSpace — MaxFi Portfolio Dashboard
Paste your MaxFi position data to generate your personal dashboard. Works for any Base chain wallet. Nothing is stored or sent anywhere.
1
Open MaxFi at maxfi.tech and go to your positions page.
2
Use the DadsDeFiSpace Claude prompt — screenshot or copy your position cards, paste into Claude with our community prompt, get back clean JSON automatically.
3
Paste JSON below and hit Generate. Your IL, APR, cashflow, risk, scenarios, and allocation render instantly.
4
Add compounding info — set autoCompound: true on positions that compound automatically. MaxFi compounds 50% to your wallet, 50% back into the position.
Field reference: pair, positionId, dex, daysOld, value, earned, uncollected, collected, apr, dpd, rebalances, inRange, autoCompound, tokenA, qtyA, entryPriceA, currentPriceA, tokenB, qtyB, entryPriceB, currentPriceB, rangeLower, rangeUpper, rangeCurrentPrice.
